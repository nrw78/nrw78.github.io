Hospital Forum connects health care leaders with data, education, tools and networking opportunities to help them achieve performance excellence and provide exceptional care in their communities.

Data

The HF Annual Survey profiles more than 6,400 U.S. hospitals. It has more than 1,000 inputs covering organization structure, service lines, staffing, expenses, physician organization structures, beds and utilization. Tap into this comprehensive data via digital and print products including the HF Guide, HF Hospital Statistics, HF DataViewer and e-Source.

Hospital Forum is dedicated to identifying solutions that help hospitals in their efforts to support the Triple Aim of better care, better health and lower cost. We start by conducting internal and external research to identify key challenges faced by the field, and then evaluate the solutions that address them. We assess products and services with rigor and award our exclusive endorsement to the solutions that excel in all areas of the evaluation process.
